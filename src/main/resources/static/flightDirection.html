Flight Direction Analysis

POST /api/algorithms/flights/direction — given a sequence of compass headings recorded at regular intervals, decides whether the plane ended up to the right, to the left, or directly along its initial course.

Comma- or space-separated integers in [0, 360), taken at regular intervals (equal-length segments assumed). Aviation convention: 0° = North, increasing clockwise. Minimum 2 readings, max 10 000.
